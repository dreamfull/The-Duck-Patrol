Lore

The Solana community was in the beginning a healthy community with good intentions. Scammers and Ruggers were unknown and yet to be seen. There was peace in the community and the people had no worries, and no fear when they decided to mint an NFT. Though this changed little by little, and the Evil was finally unveiled. More and more Rugs started happening, and this Evil almost crumbled the Solana NFT space. Even though innocent citizens suffered so much loss, they still believed that there were good people left in the space, so the space held strong and fought Evil with Good. This was all strenghtened by the old times, where Rugs did not exist and where peace and tranquility was the standard.
‍
The Solana space has now become intoxicated with thieves for far too long. Thieves that have been freely roaming and freely rugging thousands of people. The time of rugging will now come to an end, for a special group of Ducks have decided to bring Justice with their own hands. This group is called The Duck Patrol, and their goal is to make The Solana NFT space free of thieves, and welcoming for citizens. The Duck Patrol has now started to recruit members to their Duck Station, in order for them to help The Duck Patrol fight Injustice with Justice. Together we can keep the space clean of those who try to tarnish it with dishonour. In this Chaos there will be Order, and in this Injustice there will be Justice. Justice will Prevail.
